About Me


My name is Vijay Saddi and I am currently a BI Data Analyst in the Telecommunications industry. I have a BSc in Psychology from York University and a Post-Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics.

I first became interested in data after my internship at Reliance Foundation in Bombay, India. I worked in the Health Information Team, where I was involved in many projects involving heavy use of patient data. At this point I didn't have a background on performing basic analyses, but found it extremely interesting to manipulate. At this moment I knew I wanted to build a career in Data.

With the wealth of resources online, I gradually started learning the field, starting with some basic Python code and building from there on. Since then, I have completed several certificates including: Data Camp's Data Engineering career track, IBM's Data Science Specialization and Python for Data Science and Machine Learning and more. I have also applied my knowledge by completing some relevant projects such as a Spotify Song recommender, Covid-19 X-Ray Detector, and market research for a business proposal.

This is my jorney to date, with the main goal of helping to drive the industry forward through innovation.
